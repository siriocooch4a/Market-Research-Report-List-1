<p><strong>The VOC Measurement Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC measurement device market primarily includes two types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID devices utilize ultraviolet light to ionize VOCs, providing high sensitivity and quick response for detecting low concentrations, making them ideal for industrial environments. In contrast, MOS sensors detect VOCs through resistance changes in a semiconductor material when exposed to organic compounds. While MOS sensors are cost-effective and suitable for various applications, they may offer lower accuracy compared to PID devices.</p></p>
