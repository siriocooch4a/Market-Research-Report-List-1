<p><p>The airport conveyor market is primarily segmented into belt and roller conveyors. Belt conveyors are widely used for baggage handling due to their continuous surface, ensuring smooth transport and efficient sorting of luggage. They excel in moving heavy loads over long distances. Roller conveyors, on the other hand, utilize a series of rollers to facilitate the movement of items, making them ideal for quick transfers and sorting. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing operational efficiency and improving passenger flow at airports.</p></p>
