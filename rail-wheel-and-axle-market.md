<p><p>The Rail Wheel and Axle Market is categorized into two main types: rolled wheels and axles, and forged wheels and axles. Rolled wheels and axles are produced through a process involving rolling metals, resulting in efficient production and consistent quality, ideal for standard railway applications. In contrast, forged wheels and axles are created by shaping heated metal under pressure, offering superior strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-load and high-performance rail systems. Both types play crucial roles in railway infrastructure.</p></p>
