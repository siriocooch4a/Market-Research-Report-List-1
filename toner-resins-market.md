<p><ul><li>Magnetic</li><li>Non-Magnetic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The toner resins market is categorized into magnetic and non-magnetic types. Magnetic toner resins contain magnetic particles, enabling faster printing and efficient imaging for applications like magnetic printing and security features. Non-magnetic toner resins rely on electrostatic charges for imaging and are widely used in standard laser printers. Each type serves distinct purposes; magnetic resins are tailored for specialized applications, while non-magnetic ones dominate general printing needs, reflecting diverse demands in the market.</p></p>
