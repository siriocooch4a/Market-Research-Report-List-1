<p><p>Connected Parking Solutions encompass various market types, including angle, perpendicular, and parallel parking. Angle parking involves vehicles positioned at a diagonal to the curb, optimizing space in congested areas. Perpendicular parking allows cars to be parked directly facing the curb, maximizing the number of vehicles in a given area but requiring more space. Parallel parking, a common urban setting, enables vehicles to park parallel to the curb, often requiring skilled maneuvering but allowing efficient use of street space. Each type addresses specific urban parking challenges.</p></p>
