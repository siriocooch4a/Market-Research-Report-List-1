<p><p>Gap insurance encompasses several types designed to protect vehicle owners from financial losses in case of theft or total loss. Finance GAP Insurance covers the difference between the insurance payout and the remaining finance balance. Return-to-invoice GAP Insurance ensures the owner receives the original purchase price. Vehicle Replacement GAP Insurance facilitates purchasing a new model after a loss. Return-to-value GAP Insurance addresses depreciation, covering the market value difference. Other types may include variations tailored to specific consumer needs or circumstances.</p></p>
