<p><p>The intraocular lens (IOL) market comprises various types designed for cataract surgery and vision correction. Non-foldable lenses are rigid and require larger incisions for implantation. Foldable intraocular lenses, made of flexible materials, allow for smaller incisions and quicker recovery. Hydrophilic IOLs are water-loving and provide better biocompatibility, while hydrophobic lenses repel water, offering enhanced optical properties and reduced complications. Together, these segments cater to diverse patient needs and preferences in ophthalmic treatments, driving innovation in the market.</p></p>
