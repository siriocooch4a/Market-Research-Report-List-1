<p><ul><li>Thermosetting Elastomers</li><li>hermoplastic Resins (Non-Elastomeric)</li><li>Thermoplastic Elastomers (TPE)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polymeric flexible tubing market comprises three main types: thermosetting elastomers, thermoplastic resins, and thermoplastic elastomers (TPE). Thermosetting elastomers are durable, maintaining shape under heat, ideal for high-stress applications. Thermoplastic resins are non-elastomeric materials, offering rigidity and chemical resistance, used where structural integrity is critical. Thermoplastic elastomers combine the flexibility of elastomers with thermoplastic processing, providing versatility across various applications, including automotive and medical sectors, due to their ability to be molded and reprocessed easily.</p></p>
