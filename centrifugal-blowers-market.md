<p><ul><li>Multistage Centrifugal Blowers</li><li>Air Suspension Blower</li><li>Magnetic Levitation Blower</li><li>Single Stage High-Speed Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Centrifugal blowers are categorized into several types based on their design and application. Multistage centrifugal blowers increase pressure through multiple impellers, making them suitable for high-pressure requirements. Air suspension blowers utilize air bearings for reduced friction and noise. Magnetic levitation blowers employ magnetic fields for contactless operation, enhancing efficiency and lifespan. Single-stage high-speed blowers operate with a single impeller, delivering significant airflow at lower pressure, ideal for applications needing quick air movement. Each type serves specific industrial needs effectively.</p></p>
