<p><p>Step-down switching regulators, commonly known as buck converters, efficiently reduce high input voltages to lower output levels while maintaining high efficiency. They convert electrical energy by releasing stored energy in an inductor, resulting in minimal power loss. The buck controller, on the other hand, manages the operation of the buck converter, providing control signals to maintain the desired output voltage and current. Together, these components play a crucial role in powering various electronic devices and systems, from consumer electronics to industrial applications.</p></p>
