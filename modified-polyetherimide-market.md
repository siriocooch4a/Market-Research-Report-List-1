<p><p>The Modified Polyetherimide market includes two key types based on production methods: melt polycondensation and solution polymerization. Melt polycondensation involves the continuous heating of monomers to form polymers directly, offering advantages in scalability and processing efficiency. In contrast, solution polymerization entails dissolving monomers in a solvent to facilitate polymer formation, which allows for better control over molecular weight and composition. Both methods cater to diverse application needs, leading to varied performance characteristics in end-use products.</p></p>
