<p><p>The PET collection tubes market comprises various types designed for specific blood sample processing needs. Serum separating tubes facilitate the separation of serum from blood cells, crucial for biochemical testing. EDTA tubes, containing an anticoagulant, are used for complete blood counts and other hematological tests. Plasma separation tubes are designed to isolate plasma quickly for testing purposes. Additionally, other tubes may include those for specific assays, microbial cultures, or specialized testing, catering to diverse laboratory requirements.</p></p>

<p><p>The PET collection tubes market serves vital applications in both venous and capillary blood collection. Venous blood collection involves drawing blood from a vein, typically in a clinical setting, which requires high-quality tubes for accurate testing and results. Both collection methods benefit from PET tubes' excellent barrier properties and compatibility with various analyses, ensuring sample integrity and reliability.</p></p>
