<p><p>FPD transfer robots are essential for moving flat panel displays in manufacturing environments. Atmospheric robots operate in standard atmospheric conditions, utilizing mechanical arms to handle delicate panels with precision, ensuring minimal contamination. Vacuum robots, on the other hand, use suction mechanisms to securely lift and transfer displays, providing a strong hold without risk of damage. Both types are crucial for optimizing efficiency, reducing the risk of defects, and enhancing safety in the production of flat panel displays.</p></p>
