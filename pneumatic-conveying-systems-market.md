<p><ul><li>Transportation Systems</li><li>Positive Pressure Transportation Systems</li><li>Vacuum Transport System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pneumatic conveying systems facilitate efficient material transport using air pressure or vacuum. Transportation systems utilize positive pressure to push materials through pipelines, ideal for short distances and high flow rates. Positive pressure transportation systems maintain higher air pressure to rapidly move bulk materials, ensuring continuous flow with minimal blockage. In contrast, vacuum transport systems use negative pressure to pull materials, making them suitable for fragile materials or longer distances while minimizing dust and environmental exposure.</p></p>
