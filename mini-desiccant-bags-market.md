<p><p>The mini desiccant bags market primarily consists of three types: molecular sieve, activated carbon, and calcium chloride. Molecular sieves excel at moisture adsorption and are ideal for low-humidity environments. Activated carbon is effective in removing odors and volatile organic compounds, making it suitable for various applications. Calcium chloride is a hygroscopic material that absorbs moisture rapidly and is commonly used in environments prone to high humidity. Each type serves distinct purposes in protecting goods from moisture damage.</p></p>
