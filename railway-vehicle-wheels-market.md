<p><p>The railway vehicle wheels market primarily consists of two types: rolling steel wheels and cast steel wheels. In contrast, cast steel wheels are produced by pouring molten steel into molds, offering high strength and durability. Both types are crucial for ensuring smooth and efficient rail operations, catering to various railway applications and enhancing overall performance.</p></p>
