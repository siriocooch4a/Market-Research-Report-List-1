<p><p>The Molded Case Circuit Breaker (MCCB) market includes two main types: Miniature Circuit Breakers (MCBs) and MCCBs. MCBs are designed for low-voltage applications, providing protection against overloads and short circuits, typically in residential and commercial settings. MCCBs, on the other hand, cater to medium voltage applications, suitable for industrial uses, offering higher current ratings and adjustable trip settings.
