<p><strong>The Fiber-optic Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-Mode</li><li>Multi-Mode</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fiber-optic cable market is primarily segmented into two types: single-mode and multi-mode cables. Single-mode cables have a smaller core diameter, allowing only one light mode to propagate, which enables long-distance communication with minimal signal loss. In contrast, multi-mode cables have a larger core, supporting multiple light modes simultaneously, making them suitable for shorter distances and high-bandwidth applications within buildings or campuses. Each type caters to specific networking needs, influencing their demand and market dynamics.</p></p>
