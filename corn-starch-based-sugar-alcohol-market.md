<p><p>The corn starch-based sugar alcohol market comprises several key types: Sorbitol, Mannitol, Erythritol, and Maltitol, each with unique properties and applications. Erythritol is favored for its near-zero calories and minimal aftertaste. Maltitol is often used in sugar-free confections due to its similar sweetness to sugar. Other sugar alcohols may include Xylitol and Isomalt, serving diverse dietary needs.</p></p>
