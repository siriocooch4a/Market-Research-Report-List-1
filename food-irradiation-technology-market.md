<p><p>Food irradiation technology involves the use of ionizing radiation to enhance food safety and extend shelf life. The main market types are Cobalt and Electronic Accelerators. Cobalt-60 is a radioactive isotope that emits gamma rays, making it effective for sterilizing food and eliminating pathogens. Electronic accelerators, on the other hand, use high-energy electrons generated by electricity to achieve similar results. Both methods are employed in various applications, including preservation, pest control, and disinfection of food products.</p></p>
