<p><p>The steam turbine-driven generator market consists of three primary types: gas turbines, steam turbines, and water turbines. Gas turbines convert natural gas or liquid fuels into mechanical energy, suitable for high-efficiency power generation. Steam turbines utilize steam produced from boiling water to drive blades, commonly used in thermal power plants. Water turbines harness kinetic energy from flowing water, prevalent in hydroelectric facilities. Each type plays a critical role in diversifying energy sources and enhancing overall electricity generation efficiency.</p></p>
