<p><p>The carbon steel seamless pipe market is divided into two main types: cold drawn and hot rolled. Cold drawn pipes undergo a process where they are pulled through a die at room temperature, resulting in enhanced dimensional accuracy and improved mechanical properties. In contrast, hot rolled pipes are formed at high temperatures, allowing for easier shaping and larger sizes, but with less precision. Each type serves distinct applications based on strength, flexibility, and manufacturing requirements in various industries.</p></p>
