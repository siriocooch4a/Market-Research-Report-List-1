<p><p>The cryogenic expander market primarily consists of two types: single-phase expanders and two-phase expanders. Single-phase expanders operate with a single fluid phase, typically focusing on gas expansion, and are simpler in design, making them suitable for applications requiring efficient gas processing. In contrast, two-phase expanders handle both liquid and gas phases, allowing for more complex operations, such as in liquefied natural gas applications. Both types play critical roles in various industries, enhancing energy efficiency and performance.</p></p>
