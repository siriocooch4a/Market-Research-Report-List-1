<p><p>The Electric Vehicle Supply Equipment (EVSE) industry consists of three primary market types: Level 1, Level 2, and Level 3 charging. Level 1 chargers use standard household outlets, providing slow charging suitable for home use. Level 2 chargers offer faster charging and are often installed in public or commercial spaces, ideal for overnight or extended parking. Level 3 chargers, or DC fast chargers, deliver rapid charging, making them suitable for quick stops along highways, enabling longer-distance travel for electric vehicles.</p></p>
