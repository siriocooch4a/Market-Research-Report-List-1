<p><p>The medical release paper market is categorized into two main types: single-sided coated and double-sided coated. Single-sided coated release paper features a coating on one side, optimizing it for applications like medical tape and labels, where only one surface requires a smooth release. In contrast, double-sided coated release paper is treated on both sides, making it suitable for products needing adhesion on both sides, such as wound dressings or specialized adhesives in healthcare, ensuring versatility and effectiveness in medical applications.</p></p>
