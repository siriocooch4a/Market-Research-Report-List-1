<p><p>The orifice fitting market includes two primary types: dual chamber and single chamber orifice fittings. Dual chamber orifice fittings enhance flow measurement accuracy by providing two separate chambers, allowing for advanced monitoring of pressure and flow characteristics. In contrast, single chamber orifice fittings are simpler and often more cost-effective, suitable for standard applications with less complex flow requirements. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, including oil and gas, water treatment, and chemical processing, offering solutions for efficient flow control and measurement.</p></p>
